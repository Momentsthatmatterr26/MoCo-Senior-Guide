Financial Assistance & Resources

Most facilities listed are private pay. However, Maryland offers several programs that may help eligible families offset costs, including the Community Options Waiver, Senior Assisted Living Subsidy, and VA Aid & Attendance benefits for veterans.

For personalized guidance on financial assistance, contact Montgomery County Aging & Disability Services at (240) 777-3000. They can connect you with a care coordinator at no cost.
